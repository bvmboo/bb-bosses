Moon Presence

Description

The Moon Presence is a Great One in contract with Gehrman. It is presumed that it is also the source of Paleblood which is the Hunter's main objective. If met, no doubt it will try to tempt you in replacing Gehrman in order to prolong the Hunter's Dream.

Tips

  • It has an attack that will reduce your hp to 1, which in turn you can capitalize on by attacking 5-6 times.
  • Like the others, the Moon Presence is weak to bolt damage as it is a Great One.
  • His attack pattern consist mainly of a 3-attacks combo and a tail sweep.
