Mergo's Wet Nurse

Description

The Wet Nurse guards Mergo, an infant of the Phtumerian Queen (which can be seen near the elevator leading to the boss fight) and the source of the Nightmare.

Tips

  • Remain at the center of her back in order to avoid most attacks.
  • During the "mist" phase with the clone, it's best to run away until it dissipates.
