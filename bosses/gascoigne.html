Father Gascoigne

Description

Father Gascoigne is one of the hunters of Yharnam. His obsession with beast hunting has become so dire that his thirst for blood turned him into one.

Tips

  • You can use the music box given by the young girl in Yharnam to briefly stun Gascoigne.
  • Make use of the tombstones and other obstacles to protect yourself from wide axe swings.
  • Stock up on Molotov Cocktails for his second phase.
  • During his 2nd phase, distance yourself from him to prompt a dash attack that is easily parriable.
