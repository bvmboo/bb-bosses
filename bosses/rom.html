Rom, the Vacuous Spider

Description

Rom acts as a barrier to protect the commonfolk from the unseen. Creatures otherwise invisible to the eye such as the Amygdalas. Previously human, it has made a pact with Kos in exchange for these powers.

Tips

  • As a being closely related to the Great Ones, Rom is weak to bolt damage.
  • The safer method is to finish all the spiders before moving onto Rom.
  • Be wary of when it curls upwards as it will explode and most likely one-shot you.
