Darkbeast Paarl

Description

The darkbeast can be found in the depths of Yahar'gul, lying dead. It reacts as you get close to it standing up and generating electricty from its hide.

Tips

  • Paarl is very weak to blunt damage, provided you can land a powerful charged attack on it.
  • Limit his time coated in electricity by breaking his stance.
  • Take advantage of it trying to recharge as it offers a good window to attack.
