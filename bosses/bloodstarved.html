Blood-starved Beast

Description

The Blood-starved Beast is a beast draped in a red cloak of bloody skin. It is found in Old Yharnam which has been mostly burnt to the ground. It roams near the church at the bottom looking for its next victim.

Tips

  • Like all Beast-type enemies, it is weak to serrated and fire damage.
  • Combine Beast Blood Pellets, Fire Paper, and Pungent Blood Cocktails for massive damage without interruption.
  • Carry Antidotes as its attacks and second phase especially, cause poison.
