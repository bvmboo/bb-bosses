Witch of Hemwick

Description

The Witch of Hemwick lies at the end of the village, conducting rituals as she gathers the eyes of her victims in an attempt to offer to the Great Ones.

Tips

  • Ignore the Shades that spawn in the arena as more will take their place.
  • Focus on Backstabs and Visceral Attacks as the witch is slow and barely moving.
  • Be wary of the light orbs that will capture you if touched during the 2nd phase.
